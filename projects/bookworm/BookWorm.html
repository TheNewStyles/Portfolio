Bookworm Mobile App

Ideation

BookWorm is a concept for a mobile app that allows users to trade and sell books in their area. The goal was to create an easy to use application that made it fun to connect with other book lovers like you.

I started sketching up some concepts and thinking about how I would like the workflow to take place. After some trial and error I moved on to creating some wireframes.

Wireframes

Branding

Before I begin creating logos in Adobe Illustrator I like to create some moodboards for inspiration. From there I start sketching up some quick concepts with a pad and pencil. When I am happy with one of my idea I move it into Illustrator and create some vector graphics and figure out a color scheme. After I finish the branding. I use the moodboards and final logo to help me decide on the look and feel for the final high fidelity mockups.
